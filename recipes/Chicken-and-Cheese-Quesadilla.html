I could eat these every day. They taste great and are so easy to make. Plus you don't need any silverware to eat them. Just pick'em right up!

Ingredients

  • 3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into small pieces
  • 2 cups chopped onions
  • 1 green bell pepper*, chopped
  • 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp taco seasoning
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp garlic salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 4oz can diced green chilies*
  • 2-3 cups Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
  • 2-3 cups sharp chedder cheese, shredded>
  • 8 flour tortillas, soft taco sized
  • 2 Tbps butter, divided

Optional* If you don't like spicy foods, leave out the chilies. If you want it spicier, use a Jalapeno instead of a bell pepper.

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a frying pan or large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add chicken, onions and bell pepper into pan, sprinkle with taco seasoning, garlic salt, cumin, pepper and red pepper flakes, stir and cook until chicken is cooked and onions are translucent.
  3. Pour diced green chilies into pan, stir until combined and continuing cooking for a few more minutes.
  4. Add chicken mixture on one half of tortilla, add both types of cheese on top of chicken, fold tortilla over and add another tortilla to pan and repeat process.
  5. Cook over medium heat until tortillas starts to brown, a few minutes per side, then flip over and cook the other side until the cheese is melted and the quesadillas are golden brown.
  6. Remove from pan, place on a plate.
  7. Add the other tablespoon of butter to pan and repeat with the remaining tortillas and chicken mixture until all 8 quesadillas are made.
  8. Cut into wedges right before serving.
  9. Top with your favorite toppings like sour cream, green onions, cilantro and/or avocado.
  10. Most importantly, ENJOY
