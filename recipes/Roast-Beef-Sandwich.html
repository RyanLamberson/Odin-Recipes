If you're anything like me, you feel tired and distracted at work in the afternoon. The problem is you're hungry and running out of steam. A hearty roast beef sandwich is perfect for giving you the energy you need to get through the day.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp olive oil mayonnaise
  • 2 Tbsp Greek yogurt
  • 2 Tbsp prepared horseradish
  • 1 Tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, finely minced
  • 2 cups arugula
  • 8 slices multi grain bread, lightly toasted
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1‚ÅÑ2 red onion, very thinly sliced
  • 1 lb leftover garlic-rosemary roast beef, thinly sliced, or high-quality store-bought roast beef
  • 4 slices sharp Cheddar

Instructions

  1. Combine the mayo, yogurt, horseradish, Dijon, and garlic in a mixing bowl.
  2. Divide the arugula among 4 pieces of bread.
  3. Top with the tomato slices (seasoned with a pinch of salt), onion slices, roast beef, and Cheddar.
  4. Spread the top pieces of bread with a thick layer of the horseradish mayo and complete the sandwiches.
